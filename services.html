Services

Bishtography offers photography services focused on storytelling, composition, and visual atmosphere. Each project is approached with attention to detail, combining technical skill with creative direction to produce images that feel intentional and cinematic.

Toy Photography

This service focuses on creating cinematic scenes using LEGO and other small-scale figures. Each shoot is carefully planned with attention to lighting, perspective, and storytelling. The goal is to transform simple objects into dynamic visual narratives that feel larger than their physical scale.

Clients can request themed concepts, character-driven scenes, or abstract visual storytelling. Both physical setups and digital enhancement techniques (such as Blender) may be used depending on the project.

Outdoor Photography

Outdoor photography captures real environments with a focus on mood, lighting, and natural composition. Special attention is given to weather conditions, reflections, and textures to create visually engaging results.

This service is ideal for nature-based imagery, urban exploration, and lifestyle compositions that rely on natural light and authentic surroundings.

Custom Projects

Custom photography projects are available for clients with specific ideas or creative concepts. These projects are highly flexible and can include staged scenes, experimental compositions, or hybrid workflows combining photography and digital tools.

Each project is developed collaboratively, from concept planning through to final editing, ensuring the final result matches the intended vision.

Editing & Post-Production

Post-production services include colour correction, lighting adjustments, and detailed image refinement. The editing process is used not only to enhance image quality but also to strengthen mood and storytelling.

Advanced edits may include compositing, background enhancement, and visual consistency adjustments across a series of images.
