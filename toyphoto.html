Toy Photography

This collection explores storytelling through small-scale LEGO scenes, transforming simple subjects into cinematic compositions. By carefully controlling lighting, framing, and perspective, each image aims to create a sense of realism and narrative beyond the physical scale of the objects.

Featured Work

“Trapped”

Concept: A visual exploration of confinement using layered space and repetition.

Technique: Controlled framing and lighting to obscure the true setting and guide focus.

Outcome: Encourages viewers to interpret the scene beyond its physical structure.

Beach Scene

Concept: Blending physical LEGO subjects with digital environments.

Technique: Use of Blender to simulate realistic lighting and reflections.

Outcome: Creates a seamless interaction between real and virtual elements.

Process

Each project begins with a physical setup where composition and perspective are tested. Lighting is carefully adjusted to create depth and mood, often mimicking natural light sources. Selected works are enhanced using Photoshop and Blender to extend environments and refine lighting, allowing for greater control over realism while maintaining a grounded visual base.
