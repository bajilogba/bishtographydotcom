Recent Work

This image captures a wooden butterfly suspended against a rain-soaked environment, with a bridge subtly blurred in the background. The composition explores contrast between stillness and movement, as well as the calm atmosphere that follows rainfall. The natural lighting and depth of field were used to emphasise the subject while preserving a sense of place.

This piece experiments with combining physical LEGO subjects and digital environments using Blender. The beach setting allowed for exploration of lighting, reflections, and realism, blending 3D rendering with practical photography. The goal was to create a seamless interaction between real and virtual elements.

“Trapped” plays with perspective and visual illusion, placing a toy inside what appears to be an enclosed environment without immediately revealing it as a microwave. The repetition of confinement, with another figure boxed within the scene, reinforces the theme of restriction. The composition focuses on storytelling through ambiguity and controlled framing.
