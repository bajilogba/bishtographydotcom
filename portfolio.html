Portfolio

This portfolio showcases a range of work exploring both natural environments and constructed scenes. My work focuses on storytelling, lighting, and composition, often blending real-world photography with digital techniques to create more immersive visuals.

Toy Photography

Narrative-driven LEGO scenes combining practical setups with digital tools like Blender. Focused on lighting, perspective, and storytelling to create cinematic, immersive compositions.

Outdoor Photography

A collection of natural and urban environments captured with an emphasis on atmosphere, depth, and timing. Often inspired by post-rain scenes and subtle details within everyday spaces.

Process

My workflow varies depending on the subject. For outdoor photography, I focus on timing, weather, and composition to capture natural atmosphere. For toy photography, I combine physical setups with digital tools like Blender to experiment with lighting, scale, and realism. Each project involves testing multiple angles and lighting conditions before finalising the shot.

Inspiration

My work is inspired by everyday environments and the idea of transforming ordinary scenes into something visually engaging. Rain, reflections, and lighting conditions often influence my outdoor photography, while storytelling and perspective drive my toy photography. I am particularly interested in how small subjects can be presented in a way that feels large and cinematic.
